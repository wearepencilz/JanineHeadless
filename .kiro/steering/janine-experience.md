# Janine Physical + Digital Experience

## In-Store Experience

### Customer Journey

#### 1. Walk In
Customer enters the shop and sees:
- Physical menu board
- QR codes on display
- Seasonal flavour highlights
- Featured collaborations

#### 2. Scan QR Menu
Customer scans QR code and sees:
- Full flavour details
- Ingredient provenance
- Tasting notes
- Allergen information
- "In the making" photos
- Related flavours

#### 3. Explore or Order
Customer can:
- Browse the archive
- Read ingredient stories
- Order online (Skip the Line)
- Add to cart for pickup

#### 4. Order Online
Customer:
- Selects flavours
- Chooses format (soft serve, pint, etc.)
- Selects pickup time
- Pays online

#### 5. Pick Up
Customer:
- Receives confirmation
- Arrives at selected time
- Picks up order
- Scans QR on cup/cone for more info

## QR Code Placement Strategy

### In-Store
- **Menu boards** - Main flavour menu with QR per flavour
- **Counter display** - Featured flavour of the week
- **Table tents** - Seasonal specials and collaborations
- **Wall art** - Ingredient stories and farm partnerships

### On Product
- **Cups** - QR on side of cup
- **Cones** - QR on wrapper/sleeve
- **Pint containers** - QR on lid or label
- **Chocolate bars** - QR on wrapper
- **Gift boxes** - QR on packaging

### Marketing Materials
- **Event posters** - Link to event details
- **Collaboration announcements** - Link to story
- **Seasonal campaigns** - Link to collection

## Click & Collect / Skip the Line

### How It Works

#### 1. Browse Online
- View current menu
- See available pickup times
- Check flavour availability

#### 2. Build Order
- Select flavours
- Choose format (soft serve, pint, etc.)
- Add any extras (toppings, cones)
- Select pickup time

#### 3. Pay Online
- Secure checkout
- Save payment for future
- Apply Golden Spoon points
- Get confirmation

#### 4. Pickup
- Arrive at selected time
- Skip the line
- Show confirmation
- Collect order

### Benefits
- No waiting in line
- Guaranteed flavour availability
- Pre-order for events
- Golden Spoon points
- Saved preferences
