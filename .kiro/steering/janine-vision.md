# Janine Digital Platform Vision

## Core Philosophy

Janine should behave digitally like a mix of:
- **Flamingo Estate** - Ingredient storytelling and provenance
- **Noma Projects** - Experimentation and documentation
- **Seasonal Gelato Shop** - Commerce with craft focus

> Commerce is important. But the archive of flavour experimentation becomes the real brand asset.

## Four Pillars

### 1. Digital Extension of the Shop
- Help customers explore flavours before visiting
- Order ahead and skip the line
- Engage with the brand beyond the physical space

### 2. Living Archive
- Document every flavour experiment
- Track batches and iterations
- Preserve seasonal flavours and collaborations
- Build a searchable flavour history

### 3. Community Layer
- Golden Spoon membership program
- Flavour ratings and favourites
- Events and tastings
- Storytelling and engagement

### 4. Test Kitchen Interface
- Support experimentation workflow
- Batch tracking and notes
- Recipe evolution documentation
- Internal decision-making tool

## Balance Points

The platform must balance:
- **Commerce** - Shopify-powered shop with seasonal products
- **Discovery** - Flavour and ingredient exploration
- **Story** - Editorial content and brand narrative
- **Experimentation** - Test kitchen and batch documentation

## Seasonal Commerce Flow

### Spring / Summer
Primary products:
- Ice cream (pints)
- Soft serve
- Affogato
- Cold drinks

### Fall / Winter
Primary products:
- Chocolate bars
- Hot chocolate
- Pantry items
- Gift sets

## Physical + Digital Experience

### In-Store Flow
1. Walk in
2. Scan QR menu
3. See flavours with full details
4. Order online
5. Pick up

### QR Code System
QR codes appear on:
- Shop menus
- Cones and cups
- Packaging
- Event materials

Scanning opens:
- Flavour page with full details
- Ingredient provenance
- Ordering page
- Related stories

## Key Strategic Idea

The archive of flavour experimentation becomes the real brand asset - not just the commerce layer. Every batch, every ingredient, every collaboration is documented and becomes part of the brand story.
